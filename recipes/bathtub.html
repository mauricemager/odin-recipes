Iced Bathtub Water Cocktail

Description

Thirsty and feeling sweaty? This recipe kills 2 birds with one stone.

Ingredients

  • Bathtub
  • water
  • Bath soap
  • Ice cubes
  • Long drink glass
  • Lemon

Instructions

  1. Work your ass of so you become sweaty
  2. Fill up the bathtub with enough water to submerge yourself up to your belly button (very important detail!)
  3. Place yourself in hot bathtub, wash yourself with soap and relax
  4. Exit bath but do not drain the water, reserve one glass for the cocktail and one cup for pasta sauce
  5. Let glass of bathtub water cool down, then add loads of ice
  6. Garnisch with a slice of lemmen and enjoy
