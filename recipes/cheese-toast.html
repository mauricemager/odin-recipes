Grilled Cheese Toast

Description

This recipe makes a delicious grilled cheese toast that will make your mouth water!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of choice
  • Grated Cheddar
  • Grated Gouda
  • Siracha
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Take one slice of bread and smear with mayonnaise
  2. Put the slice with mayonnaise facing down in grill pan
  3. Add mixture of cheese on top of bread
  4. Sprinkle with salt & pepper
  5. Close the toast with bread and smear top with mayonnaise
  6. Press down with spatula and grill until golden and all cheese melted while flipping sides
  7. Make a sauce with 2 parts mayonnaise and 1 part Siracha
  8. Cut the toast diagonal and complement with siracha-mayo
  9. Enjoy your delicious grilled cheese toast!
