Sliced Banana

Description

Did your mother never teach you how to cut up a bananan? Wait no longer and follow this very detailed recipe and enjoy the food of the gods!

Ingredients

  • One banana
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Appetite

Instructions

  1. Take your banana and peel of the skin the monkey way
  2. Place the banana on your cutting board and slice in 1cm wide slices with the knife
  3. Place your delicous meal in a fancy manner and enjoy with appetite
